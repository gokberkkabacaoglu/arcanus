\begin{abstract}


The basic idea behind our approach is to convert (gravitational)
 potential energy in this buoyant air layer  
to kinetic energy in an anchored, columnar vortex, and to use that
 kinetic energy to drive a vertical-axis turbine, coupled to an electric
 generator. 

This work is supported by the Department of Energy [Advanced Research
 Projects Agency-Energy] under Award Number [DE-FOA-0000670].
