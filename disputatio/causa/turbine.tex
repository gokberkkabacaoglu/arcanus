\section{Turbine}

The normal in the blade's velocity direction is, 
\begin{equation}
n_B = \frac{u_B}{||u_B||}. 
\end{equation}
While the normal in the fan vertical direction is set, and is typically, 
\begin{equation}
n_v = \left(0,0,1\right)
\end{equation}
e.g. pointing ``up''.
